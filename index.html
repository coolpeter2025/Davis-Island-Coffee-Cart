Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our mobile coffee catering services in Davis Islands and Tampa Bay? Find answers to common questions below.

How far in advance should I book Delightful Bean for my event?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for most events, and 3-6 months ahead for wedding dates during peak season in Davis Islands. This ensures we can secure your date and have adequate time to customize your coffee experience.

What areas around Davis Islands do you serve?

We proudly serve Davis Islands and surrounding areas including Tampa, South Tampa, Hyde Park, Harbour Island, Bayshore, Channelside, and throughout Hillsborough County.

What's included in your mobile coffee bar service?

Our standard package includes our elegant mobile coffee cart, professional barista(s), premium coffee beans, specialty milk options, flavorings, all necessary equipment, cups, napkins, stirrers, and basic condiments. We also offer customizable add-ons including branded cups, specialty syrups, and coffee-based signature drinks.

How much space do you need for your coffee cart setup?

Our standard coffee cart setup requires approximately 8x8 feet of space. We also need access to a standard electrical outlet. For larger events, we may recommend additional space for queue management.

Do you offer any non-coffee options?

Yes! While we specialize in premium coffee beverages, we also offer hot chocolate, chai tea lattes, matcha lattes, and a selection of artisanal teas to accommodate non-coffee drinkers at your Davis Islands event.
