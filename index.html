Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book Delightful Bean for my event in Davis Island?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for most events in Davis Island, and 3-6 months ahead for wedding dates during peak season. This ensures we can secure your date and have adequate time to customize your coffee experience.

What areas around Davis Island do you serve?

We proudly serve Davis Island and surrounding areas including Tampa, Hyde Park, Harbour Island, Bayshore, and throughout Hillsborough County.

What's included in your mobile coffee bar service for Davis Island events?

Our standard package includes our elegant mobile coffee cart, professional barista(s), premium coffee beans, specialty milk options, flavorings, all necessary equipment, cups, napkins, stirrers, and basic condiments. We also offer customizable add-ons for your Davis Island event including branded cups, specialty syrups, and coffee-based signature drinks.

How much space do you need for your coffee cart setup in Davis Island?

Our standard coffee cart setup requires approximately 8x8 feet of space on a flat surface. We also need access to a standard 110 volt electrical outlet. If power is not available, we can provide a generator for an additional cost.

Do you offer any non-coffee options for Davis Island events?

Yes! While we specialize in premium coffee beverages, we also offer hot chocolate, chai tea lattes, matcha lattes, and a selection of artisanal teas to accommodate non-coffee drinkers at your Davis Island event.

Do you provide insurance?

Yes! We carry general liability insurance to cover unforeseen events.

What's your booking & cancellation policy?

We require a 50% deposit to confirm your booking. The remaining payment is due by the event date. Refunds are provided if you cancel 90 days from your event date and 50% of the deposit if you cancel more than 60 days from your event. Since our seasons book in advance, it’s hard to fill late cancellations so, unfortunately, cancellations made within 60 days of your event will not be refunded.

What happens if my event gets rained out?

We can not accommodate cancellations due to rain. We will work with you to find the best available option to provide cover for the bar in the event of inclement weather.
